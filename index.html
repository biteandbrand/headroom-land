01 How it works

The opportunity is in the grid before it's in the ground.

Under Connections Reform, battery capacity is rationed by zone and the pipeline already runs far ahead of the 2030 allowance. The room that opens up comes from positions that stall — accepted projects that quietly miss their connection dates. We find those first.

STEP 01

Screen the grid

Every GB grid supply point, ranked for where stalled capacity is most likely to clear — overlaid with its Clean Power 2030 zone position.

STEP 02

Point the search

A developer picks the regions and zones that fit their build strategy. That selection is the cue — we don't chase substations the whole market can already see.

STEP 03

Originate the land

We approach owners directly, off-market, and return parcels with engagement already underway — not a shortlist you'd have to work yourself.
